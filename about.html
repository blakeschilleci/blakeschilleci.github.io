More About Me

Travel

I really enjoy traveling and experiencing new things. I especially enjoy sharing the new experiences with loved ones. Plus, traveling usually means good food and beverages! I have not traveled internationally, but it's high on my bucket list to get out of the country to explore!

Sports

I grew up playing baseball and was lucky enough to play through college! After finishing college, baseball season has me focused on rooting for my beloved Atlanta Braves. And to get my "competitive" itch scratched, I play golf with my dad and buddies!

Music

Music has always been a major part of my everyday life. Through high school, I played in the drumline for the marching band and loved every minute. It opened up so many parts of the music world that I still love today. If you open my Spotify playlists you'll find Snarky Puppy, Cory Wong, Larnell Lewis, Scary Pockets and more Jazz/Fusion/Pocket bands.
